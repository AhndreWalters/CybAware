About CybAware

CybAware is a project developed by students at T.A. Marryshow Community College as part of the Project Design & Management course (PMT226). Our mission is to make cybersecurity education accessible and engaging for students.

With increasing cyber threats targeting young people, we recognized the need for practical, hands-on learning tools. CybAware addresses this by transforming complex security concepts into interactive gameplay.

The game is designed to be used in classroom settings or for individual learning, helping students develop essential digital safety skills that will protect them online.
